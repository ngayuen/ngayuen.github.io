My favorite game

My favorite game is named: "Counter-Strike:Global Offensive"

I like to play this game with two of my friends: Jack Zhang and Louie Bao. They are all very good at this game, and they also enjoy playing with me.

You might wonder why I told you that story about our somewhat naïve attempt to qualify for the first Major tournament. For me that’s the core of what CS:GO is: it’s competition at its purest.

So that is why I like this game so much and why this game is my favorite game.
